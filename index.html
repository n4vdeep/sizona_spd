Sizona Property Development

An Award Winning Developer


Sizona Property Development is an award-winning property development company specialising in London Mews houses and general refurbishment.

We are also experts in Contract Project and Site management. SPD has been presented with numerous awards which reflects the 25 years of expertise that covers sourcing, purchasing, refurbishment, sales and lettings. Our design partners can create the perfect re-design and new builds.

Other aspects we cover is advice on property investment, securing lease extensions, marketing and general property management.
